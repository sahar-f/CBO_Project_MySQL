Management System for Food Drives

Program Functionalities:

Events

Click on the events tab to view all, add, edit, delete events.

You can also view all people going to a specific event and the event's details

under the 'Event' -> 'Event Information tab'.

People

Click on the people tab to view all, add, edit, delete people attending food drives.

You can also view all the events a specific person is registered for and the person's details

under the 'People' -> 'People Information' tab.

Register a Person for an Event

To register a person for an event, please select the 'Add to Event' tab and enter the requested

information.

NOTE: Events have a maximum capacity and do run out of space!
